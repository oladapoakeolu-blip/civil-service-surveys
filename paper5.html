Welcome & Research Consent

Please read before you begin. Participation is voluntary and fully anonymous.

Before you begin:

  • This survey is conducted for academic research purposes only — it is not a staff evaluation, government inspection, or performance assessment.
  • Your responses are completely anonymous — no name, staff number, or any identifying information is collected at any point.
  • There are no right or wrong answers — please respond based on your genuine personal experience.
  • Participation is entirely voluntary — you may stop at any point without any consequence whatsoever.
  • If you appear to be experiencing high burnout, information about Employee Assistance Programmes (EAP) may be offered by the enumerator — this is completely optional.
  • Results will only ever be published as group-level statistics — no individual response will ever be identified or attributed.
