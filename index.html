Participant Rights & Data Protection

Your participation in any of these surveys is entirely voluntary. Before you begin, please note:

  • All surveys are for academic research purposes only — not government inspections, staff evaluations, or performance assessments of any kind.
  • Your responses are completely anonymous — no name, staff number, email address, or identifying information is collected at any point.
  • Data will only ever be published as group-level statistical results — no individual response will ever be identifiable in any publication.
  • You may stop at any time without any consequence, and you may skip any question you prefer not to answer.
  • Findings will be submitted to peer-reviewed academic journals and may be shared with public service reform bodies to inform evidence-based policy.
